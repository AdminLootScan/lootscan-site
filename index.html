Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

LootScan ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Data: LootScan accesses your device camera solely for scanning coins, currency, and bullion. Images captured during scanning are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers. When on-device OCR cannot process an image with sufficient confidence, the image is sent to a third-party AI vision API (Anthropic Claude) for processing. These images are sent via encrypted connection and are not retained by the API provider after processing.

Local Storage: User preferences, scan history, ignored items, and custom key dates are stored locally on your device using the app's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Precious Metals Prices: The app fetches current spot prices from third-party APIs. These requests do not include any personally identifiable information.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following: your name, email address, phone number, location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not serve advertisements.

Third-Party Services

When on-device OCR is insufficient, images are sent to the Anthropic API for vision-based text recognition. This data is transmitted securely and is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. No other third-party services receive user data.

Data Security

All API communications use HTTPS encryption. Image data sent for processing is not stored after the response is generated. Local data on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

LootScan does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
