Everything a Stacker Needs

📈

Home — Spot Prices

Live prices for gold, silver, platinum, and palladium. Melt value calculators for constitutional silver, copper pennies, and gold by weight. Track the gold-to-silver ratio at a glance.

🪙

Stack — Portfolio Tracker

Add gold, silver, copper, platinum, and palladium holdings. Track quantity, weight, cost basis, and real-time gain or loss. Organize with container tracking and import/export via CSV.

📚

Albums — Coin Checklists

Track your coin album collections by denomination and design type. Mark each coin as have, need, or upgrade. Built-in presets for popular US coin series.

🔎

Mintage — Reference Data

Look up US coin mintage numbers across 8 denominations. Identify key dates and semi-keys with interactive charts, lists, and a quick-lookup tool.

🌐

Coin Info — World Coins

Look up precious metal content and melt value of non-standard coins from around the world, including fractional bullion and historical coins.
